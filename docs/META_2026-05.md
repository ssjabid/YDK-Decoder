## Kewl Tune — *the deck to beat*
**Engine (real):** Kewl Tune Reco (89392810), Clip (43904702), Cue (16387555),
Mix (16509007), Rotary (17209452) · Synchro tuners: RS (15665977), B2B (65961304),
Remix (88170262), Track Maker (42781164), Loudness War (41069676) · JJ "Kewl Tune"
(14442329), Kewl Tune Synchro (78058681), Playlist (40847034).
- **How it wins:** chains Synchro-Tuners to generate card knowledge ("perfect
  information") and grind out a board of negates; **Kewl Tune RS negates a face-up
  card**. Side tech seen: Hecahands Godos, Prohibition.
- **Chokepoints (stop these):** their *first* Synchro-Tuner search/Special Summon —
  Ash/Imperm the Reco or the link into RS. Droll hurts the hand-knowledge loop.
- **Going first:** end on a negate they must play through; bait RS with a low-value
  activation before your real play.
- **Going second:** they sit on Synchro negates — **Dark Ruler No More / Forbidden
  Droplet** turn them off; then push. Don't over-extend into RS.
- **Confidence: medium** (engine confirmed; exact ratios/side unknown).

## Branded / Despia — *tier 1 fusion*
**Engine (real):** Aluber the Jester of Despia (62962630), Branded Fusion (44362883),
Albion the Branded Dragon (87746184), Branded in Red (82738008), Branded in White
(34995106), Despia Theater (99543666), Despian Tragedy (36577931), Comedy (90179822),
Guiding Quem (45883110), Masquerade the Blazing Dragon (6855503). Bosses:
**Mirrorjade the Iceblade Dragon** (non-targeting banish each turn), Guardian Chimera,
Albion the Sanctifire / Lubellion line; Despian Proskenion (18666161), Luluwalilith.
- **How it wins:** Fusion-summons recurring bodies; ends on **Mirrorjade** (banish
  disruption) + backrow (Branded Banishment/Beast). Grinds with GY recursion.
- **Chokepoints:** Ash **Branded Fusion** / Aluber's search; **Ghost Belle** the GY
  recursion. Bystials hurt their DARK/LIGHT GY plays.
- **Going first:** Mirrorjade alone is strong; back it with a handtrap.
- **Going second:** **Feather Duster / Lightning Storm** their backrow, then remove
  Mirrorjade with non-destruction (it survives battle/floats) — bounce or banish it.
- **Confidence: medium-high** (well-established; some new BLZD cards added).

## Dracotail — *tier 1 fusion dragons*
**Engine (real):** Faimena (1498449), Lukias (75003700), Mululu (7375867),
Pan (44482554), Phryxul (84477320), Urgula (70871153) · Fusions: Arthalion (33760966),
Gulamel (79755671), Shaulas (42125140) · Ketu (6153210) / Rahu (32548318) Dracotail.

- **Confidence: low** (new archetype — confirm specifics from a yugiohmeta guide).

## Mitsurugi — *Ritual control*
**Engine (real):** Rituals: Ame no Murakumo (19899073), Ame no Habakiri (13332685),
Futsu no Mitama (55397172) · Aramasa (40543231), Kusanagi (82782870), Saji (18176525),
Wousu (76948970) · Mitsurugi Ritual (81560239), Magatama (76725398), Mirror (49721684),
Prayers (45171524), Great Purification (17954937, trap), Tempest (49604192, trap).
- **How it wins:** Ritual Summons recurring DARK warriors, floats + grinds with traps;
  often paired with **Ryzeal** for an Xyz package.
- **Chokepoints:** Ash the Magatama/Prayers search; their traps (Great Purification /
  Tempest) are the disruption — bait or clear them.
- **Going second:** clear backrow first (Duster/Storm), then push through Ritual bodies.
- **Confidence: low-medium** (engine confirmed; exact build varies, often + Ryzeal).

## Ryzeal — *Xyz combo (frequently Ryzeal Mitsurugi)*
**Engine (real):** Sword (35844557), Star (84433129), Ice (8633261), Ext (34022970),
Node (72238166), Palm (61116514) Ryzeal · Xyz: **Ryzeal Detonator** (34909328),
**Duo Drive** (7511613) · Plugin (60394026), Cross (6798031), Mass Driver (53276089, trap).
- **How it wins:** Level-4 Xyz spam into Detonator/Duo Drive + generic R4 toolbox
  (often Fiendsmith); detaches for disruption/advantage.
- **Chokepoints:** Ash/Imperm the first Ryzeal search/Special Summon; Droll caps the
  search chains.
- **Going second:** they're combo-heavy — handtraps matter more than board breakers
  game 1; side breakers for their backrow build.
- **Confidence: low-medium.**
